Hello, world!

DanceUtils is a collection of apps to help dance events run more smoothly. Well, for now it's just a single app for helping auditions run more smoothly. But it'll expand more in the future (probably) and here's your chance to get in on the ground floor. Get started by clicking the big red button.

Sign in with Google

1. Create Your Event

You'll be asked to enter the event name, the levels to audition for and the list of students. You can optionally add a custom validation function so your instructors and volunteers will know when students are done auditioning.

2. Go Mobile

Once you've created your event, you'll get a 5-digit access code. If you visit this site from a mobile phone, you can enter that code to see and judge the students. Give the code to your instructors and volunteers and all the information will sync instantly.
